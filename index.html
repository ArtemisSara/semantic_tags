Nevermore: The Edgar Allan Poe Depot

About_Edgar

Was he a genius or a madman? Perhaps a little bit of both. It could be argued that we all possess a little bit of this and a little bit of that from time to time. Edgar, however, had a claim on more than his fair share.

Evidence

Poe quarreled with his foster father John Allan over the funds for his education. Like any young man with a chip on his shoulder would do this spurred him onto enlisting in the Army...under an assumed name. But was that madness or genius?

Probably Genius

As a result of joining the army he was able to kickstart his career in literature. How's that for genius over madness?
